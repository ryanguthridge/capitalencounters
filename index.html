Our Mission

Capital Encounters was founded after a couple friends started reading the casual encounters section of CraigsList after a night of mexican food and drinking. The idea behind this podcast to present the absurd, funny, and interesting nature of these posts. Everything on the this site and found on the Podcast was taken from a real post found on Craigslist. In all situations, identifying information, voice, name, or contact information will be omitted or changed to protect the identity of all involved.
